# Formula 1 2017: Mercedes vs Ferrari Constructor War

**OVERVIEW**
This power BI dashboard analyzes the 2017 Formula 1 Season through the lens of the constructor championship battle between Mercedes and Ferrari
The project explores team performance, driver standings, podium trends, race wins, and consistency across the season, highlighting how Mercedes secured the championship over a strong Ferrari challenge

**OBJECTIVES**
- Compare championship performance between Mercedes and Ferrari
- Analyze constructor standings and season points
- Evaluate podium consistency across leading teams and drivers
- Identify race wins leaders and overall driver standings
- Measure team efficiency through average finishing positions
- Highlight racecraft through average finishing positions
- Highlight racecraft through positions gained across the season

**KEY INSIGHTS**
- Mercedes won the 2017 constructor championship with 668 points, outperforming Ferrari on consistency and result.
- Lewis Hamilton led the season in race victories with 9 wins.
- Podium finishes were highly competitive with Lewis Hamilton, Sebastian Vettel, and Valtteri Bottas tied on 13 podiums       each.
- Average finishing positons highlighted the operational strength of top constructors.
- Racecraft metrics showed which drivers consistently outperformed their starting grid positions

**TOOLS USED**
